Siyosat va Regulyatsiya

Moliya Markazlari Rivojlanishida Davlat Roli

6 Ta Asosiy Siyosat Yo'nalishi

1. Qonunchilik Bazasi
  • Property huquqlari: Investorlar mulkining himoyasi
  • Shartnoma huquqi: Xalqaro standartlar (English law, NY law)
  • Bankruptcy qonunlari: Shaffof va adolatli bankrotlik
  • Anti-corruption: Korrupsiyaga qarshi kuchli choralar
2. Soliq Siyosati
  • Raqobatbardosh stavkalar: 15-25% korporativ soliq
  • Double taxation avoidance: Ikki tomonlama shartnomalar
  • Capital gains tax: Past yoki yo'q (Dubai, Singapore)
  • VAT/GST: Moliyaviy xizmatlarga zero-rated
3. Kadrlar Siyosati
  • Talent visa: Yuqori malakali mutaxassislarga oson visa
  • Education: Moliya bo'yicha kuchli universitetlar
  • Professional certification: CFA, ACCA, CPA dasturlari
  • Multiculturalism: Xalqaro mutaxassislar uchun ochiq muhit
4. AML/KYC Standartlari
  • FATF recommendations: 40 ta tavsiya amalga oshirish
  • CDD/EDD: Customer due diligence protokollari
  • Suspicious transaction reporting: STR tizimi
  • Sanctions compliance: OFAC, UN sanctions rioya
5. Pul-Valyuta Siyosati
  • Exchange rate stability: Barqaror valyuta kursi
  • Capital account liberalization: Kapital harakatlanishi erkinligi
  • Forex reserves: Etarli tashqi valyuta zaxiralari
  • Convertibility: To'liq konvertatsiya qilinadigan valyuta
6. Innovatsiya Siyosati
  • Regulatory sandbox: Yangi texnologiyalarni test qilish
  • Fintech licensing: Raqamli bank, crypto litsenziyalari
  • R&D incentives: Tadqiqot uchun soliq imtiyozlari
  • Open banking: API va data sharing standartlari

Regulyatsiya Modellari

Unified Regulator Model

Misol: UK (FCA), Singapore (MAS), Japan (FSA)

Xususiyati: Bitta organ barcha moliyaviy xizmatlarni tartibga soladi

Afzalliklar:
  • Yagona standartlar va qoidalar
  • Cross-sector muammolarni hal qilish oson
  • Investorlar uchun tushunarli
Kamchiliklar:
  • Juda katta va murakkab tashkilot
  • Sector-specific tajriba yetishmasligi
Twin Peaks Model

Misol: Australia (APRA + ASIC), Netherlands

Xususiyati: 2 ta regulator - prudential va conduct

Afzalliklar:
  • Aniq mas'uliyat taqsimoti
  • Specialized expertise
  • Consumer protection'ga e'tibor
Kamchiliklar:
  • Ikki organ o'rtasida koordinatsiya kerak
  • Ba'zan overlap muammolari
